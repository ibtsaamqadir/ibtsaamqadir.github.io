About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Biomedical Engineering at Purdue University, specializing in computational medical image analysis. My research involves exploring disease trajectories in pancreatic diseases using advanced deep learning methodologies and integration of multimodal clinical data. My research interests include medical image analysis, including radiology and histopathology, deep learning, multimodal AI, and biosignal processing, aiming to enhance early diagnosis and improve patient outcomes through clinical translation.
